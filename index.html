Amplitude invites you

Please join us for an evening at Riverside Golf Club.

Cocktails, nine holes, and a private dinner at one of Chicago's most historic golf venues — with a small group of the city's digital and technology leaders.

Riverside Golf Club has been a Chicago institution since 1893. 125 acres along the Des Plaines River, 12 miles from the Loop. Membership only.

Space is limited to twelve guests.
